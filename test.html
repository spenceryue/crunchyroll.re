Design Direction
    • A panel stays fixed on the screen, covers 70% of the area.
    • Support preview panels stay on the right in secondary column with 20-30% available width.
    • Scrolling switched panels, you can click to change panels as well.
    • Scrolling in the content of a panel does not switch panel.
    • On mobile, the support pane is gone largely, instead only titles of panes show up in the background, like in windows phone.
    • Swiping left and right changes panes.
    • clicks take effort, so they must mean something (every inch will be used)
    • click and drag to meta-scroll (a new UI feature)
    • click and release close to a thing and it will be clicked (optimistic click targeting with visual glow feedback)
    • hover to left/right 15% of screen (where a floating menu bar would be), this scrolls left/right, and a new pane appears in the gap
    • new meaning to double-click. double click will follow through. single click will expand a semi-transparent stack.
